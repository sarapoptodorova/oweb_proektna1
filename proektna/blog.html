The best recipe for Christmas cookies

Published on 20/11/2022

I've found the best and easiest recipe for tasty Christmas cookies, which is perfect for beginner bakers like me. They are so simple to make and so fun to decorate!
All you need is 225g of butter, 1 cup white sugar, 1.5 tbsp vanilla extract, 1 large egg, 3 cups flour, 3/4 tbsp baking powder and 3/4 tbsp salt. Preheat the oven to 180℃. Beat the butter and sugar then add the egg and vanilla and beat until combined. Add flour, baking powder and salt. Dust your work surface with flour and scrape the dough out of the bowl. Cut it in half and shape it into 2 discs.
Use cookie cutters to press out shapes. Bake for 10 minutes, until pale golden. Wait for the cookies to cool and decorate them as you wish.
Bon appetit!

Christmas tree cupcakes

Published on 12/12/2021

You will impress all of your guests with these adorable cupcakes. All you need is 150g of butter, 150g sugar, 3 eggs, 1 tbsp vanilla bean paste, 175g self raising flour and 100g gold or white fondant. For the buttercream you will need 300g butter, 500g icing sugar 1tbsp vanilla bean paste and green food coloring.
Preheat the oven to 200℃ and beat the butter and sugar until creamy. Add the eggs and the vanilla and beat again. Sift the flour into the batter and mix to combine. Divide the batter between the cases, filling them up halfway. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden. Roll out the fondant and stamp out 12 stars.
For the buttercream, mix the butter sugar and vanilla in a bowl. Add the green food coloring. Spoon the buttercream in a piping bag and pipe swirls over the cupcakes creating the tree shape. Top the trees with the fondant stars and decorate with sprinkles, sugar balls or some chocolate.
Enjoy!
